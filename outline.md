# Outline — Unreal Reels: A Practitioner's Guide

Working table of contents. Kept in sync with `chapters/`.

## Front matter
- `00-frontmatter.md` — title, copyright.
- `00-introduction.md` — what Unreal Reels is; the two laws; the three ways in; how to read this book.

## Part I — Foundations (why the pipeline is shaped the way it is)
1. **The Two Laws** — audio is the master clock; every stage is phase-gated. Why these two rules make quality controllable instead of hoped-for.
2. **The Beat Sheet** — `beat_sheet.json`, the universal spine. One beat = one narrated unit = one visual. Why one schema lets every tool compose.
3. **Storyboard-First** — the reels engine: segment → audio → references → stills → video → overlay. Curate at every gate.

## Part II — Making videos (one engine, five lenses)
4. **Songbird** — music videos, lyric videos, and beat-synced dance reels (`lyric-match`, `songbird-dance`). Includes kids' language-learning music videos.
5. **Bios** — narrated mini-biographies over B-roll; one figure, one life.
6. **Explainer** — MinutePhysics-style sketch videos with `bears-doodles`; scout a book for candidates.
7. **From Chapter to Lecture** — the three-stage lecture pipeline: `lecture-assets` → `slide-deck` → `deck-lecture`. Chapter in, narrated lecture out.

## Part III — Scale & trust (150 books, one afternoon each)
8. **Silent Mode** — rough drafts at scale; the triage layer; the three modes (agent/API/handoff); QC scores.
9. **Fidelity** — the grounding check and the shared fact commons; how a lecture stays faithful to a fact-checked book.
10. **The Look** — the brutalist design system and progressive disclosure; no static screen for 30 seconds.
11. **The Ecosystem** — AI+1 writes the book, `facts/` holds the truth, Unreal Reels makes the video; verified-artifact gates tie them together.
12. **Making It Yours** — refinement, voices, and shipping; how two experts diverge from one base into two courses.

## Back matter
- `99-back-matter.md` — acknowledgments, about the author, references.
