# Back Matter

## Acknowledgments

This book documents a system built by many hands and models across many sessions — the Unreal Reels pipeline, the AI+1 book-generation system, and the shared fact commons that ties them together. It is, fittingly, a candidate for its own pipeline: a book about turning books into video, which can itself be turned into video.

## About the author

Nik Bear Brown builds tools that let one person plus AI agents make things neither could make alone — books, courses, and the videos that carry them. He teaches at Northeastern University.

## A closing note on the two laws

If you remember nothing else: audio is the master clock, and the human signs every gate. Everything else in this book is an elaboration of those two sentences. Hold them, and the pipeline will make sense the next time you meet it — and so will whatever it grows into next.
